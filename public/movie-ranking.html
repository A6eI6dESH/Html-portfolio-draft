The Movies that I fell for

My top 3 movies of all-time.

  1. Life of Pie

    A young man who survives a disaster at sea is hurtled into an epic journey of adventure and discovery. While cast away, he forms an unexpected connection with another survivor: a fearsome Bengal tiger.

  2. Josee, the Tiger and the Fish

    After befriending a marine biology student, a sarcastic artist living with paraplegia begins to experience the world beyond her home.

  3. La La Land

    When Sebastian, a pianist, and Mia, an actress, follow their passion and achieve success in their respective fields, they find themselves torn between their love for each other and their careers.
